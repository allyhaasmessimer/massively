My name is Ally Haas Messimer

Hello! I am a full stack software engineer with a unique background in biotechnology. After earning my degree in biotechnology, I worked as a lab technician, purifying proteins and conducting research on allergens. However, after moving to a small town with no biotech opportunities, I started learning to code to stay busy. I soon realized that all the things I loved about being a lab tech, such as attention to detail, good documentation, and experimenting, could be found in coding as well. Since then, I have been building software applications, developing and maintaining web applications using a range of technologies such as JavaScript, React, Node.js, and SQL. My experience in biotechnology has given me a strong foundation in problem-solving and critical thinking, which I apply to my work in software engineering.
